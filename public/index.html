Anti-Adblock Killer Continued

Helps you keep your Ad-Blocker active, when you visit a website and it asks you to disable.

Composed of a Userscript «AakScript» written in javascript and a few filter lists «AakList» designed for the Ad-Blocker you use. They are complementary and unlock different website.

You can report a issue or contribute to the project by visiting our «GitLab» repository.
